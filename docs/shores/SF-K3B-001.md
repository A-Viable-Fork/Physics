## 1. The two channels, in one variable

Both channels are already stated in terms of the same Karch-Randall geometry. Fix notation to
`u_b = z_0/L` (K3-prime's and the audits' own variable) throughout.

**C23-SDC's channel (kinematic).** The geodesic distance in moduli space, using `z_0` as the
modulus coordinate:

    d(z_0) ~ M_Pl ln(z_0/L) = M_Pl ln(u_b)

SDC exponential decay: `m_KK ~ exp(-d/M_Pl)`. This is a *kinematic* statement: given some value of
`u_b`, it says what the geodesic distance and KK mass are. It does not, on its own, say what makes
`u_b` move.

**The K3 audits' channel (dynamical).** PK-AUD-K3-002 Section 6, carried in `k3-prime` part (iii)
and `im.goldberger-wise-effective-tension`: brane-localized vacuum energy enters the junction
condition as `T_eff = T_3 + V(v(F_Q))`, and the static position responds as

    delta u_b = (kappa_5^2 L / 6) cosh^2(u_b) delta V

This is a *dynamical* statement: given a perturbation `delta V` (sourced, per K3-prime part (iii),
by the state-dependent VEV `v(F_Q)`), it says how much `u_b` moves. It does not, on its own, say
what that motion means for the SDC/KK-tower observable.

The two statements are complementary by construction: one is d(u_b), the other is du_b/dV. The
question the gap poses is whether composing them is legitimate, i.e. whether "the thing whose
position C23-SDC's geodesic distance is a function of" and "the thing whose position the audits'
backreaction relation moves" are the same u_b under the same dynamics, or two different objects
that only coincidentally share a symbol.

## 2. Composing the chain

Differentiating C23-SDC's own formula: `dd/du_b = M_Pl / u_b`. Composing with the audits'
backreaction relation by the chain rule:

    delta d = (dd/du_b) delta u_b = (M_Pl / u_b) (kappa_5^2 L / 6) cosh^2(u_b) delta V

This is the reconciliation, stated as a single formula: it is well-defined exactly when both
relations describe the same `u_b`, which section 1 established they structurally do (both are the
Karch-Randall brane's own position variable, `z_0/L`, in the same warped-product geometry PK-AUD-
K3-002 section 3.2 derives once and both K3-prime and C23-SDC inherit).

**Near-critical asymptotics, at the framework's own physical working point.** K3-prime part (ii)
gives the physical (dS-sliced) background's deviation from critical as `(L/l_dS)^2 ~ 10^-64`. Using
the dS mirror relation `T_eff = (6/kappa_5^2 L) coth(u_b)` (the same import), and
`coth(u_b) - 1 ~ 2 exp(-2 u_b)` for large `u_b`, solving `2 exp(-2 u_b) ~ 10^-64` gives

    u_b ~ 74  (order-of-magnitude; K3-prime's own "approximately 10^-64" is itself an
               order-of-magnitude figure, so this is not claimed to more precision than that)

At this `u_b`, `cosh^2(u_b) ~ 5 x 10^63` (direct evaluation). Substituting into the composed
formula:

    delta d ~ (M_Pl / 74) (kappa_5^2 L / 6) (5 x 10^63) delta V

The `cosh^2(u_b)` enhancement, once carried through the chain rule to `d` (and hence to the
observable KK mass via `m_KK ~ exp(-d/M_Pl)`), is not a modest correction: at the framework's own
near-critical working point, an extremely small `delta V` produces an enormous `delta d`. This
directly explains, quantitatively, why K3-prime part (iii) names `|V| ~ 3L/(kappa_5^2 l^2)` as
"significant": with `l = L cosh(u_b)`, this threshold is `3/(kappa_5^2 L cosh^2(u_b))`, the same
`cosh^2(u_b)` appearing in the denominator, i.e. the threshold at which `delta u_b` (and hence
`delta d`) becomes order-one is parametrically tiny exactly where the enhancement is large. The two
numbers were independently derived (K3-prime part (iii) from the audits' own backreaction formula
directly; this section's `delta d` from composing that formula with C23-SDC's own geodesic
distance) and are mutually consistent to within the stated order-of-magnitude precision.

## 3. Three candidate resolutions, with probability estimates, before committing

**Candidate 1: same channel, one causal chain (kinematics + dynamics).** C23-SDC's geodesic-
distance formula and the audits' backreaction relation are the same `u_b`'s two faces: the audits
supply the dynamical law (what moves `u_b`, and how much, given a vacuum-energy perturbation
sourced by `v(F_Q)`), C23-SDC supplies the kinematic consequence (what a given `u_b` means for the
geodesic distance and hence the KK mass). Composing them, as section 2 does, is not an assumption
but the correct reading of two partial descriptions of one object. Estimated probability: **0.55**.
The shared variable (`u_b`, the same Karch-Randall warp-factor position in both), the shared
geometry (both trace to PK-AUD-K3-002's single master warped-product equation), and the clean
algebraic composition (section 2's formula is well-defined and internally consistent with K3-
prime's own independently-stated threshold) all favor this reading.

**Candidate 2: kinematics and dynamics are orthogonal; no coupling needed.** C23-SDC's SDC-
compliance claim needs only the geodesic-distance-as-a-function-of-`u_b` relation; it never claimed
to derive what moves `u_b`, and does not need to. The audits' backreaction relation answers a
different question (what sources `u_b` motion) that C23-SDC's own argument is agnostic to. Under
this reading, the `cosh^2` enhancement is real but irrelevant to C23-SDC's own SDC-compliance
argument, which holds for any `u_b` however it got there. Estimated probability: **0.20**. This is
not wrong, exactly: C23-SDC's SDC-compliance argument (that a large `u_b` gives a large `d` gives a
small `m_KK`) is genuinely `u_b`-source-agnostic. But "orthogonal" undersells what section 2 shows:
once a driving mechanism for `u_b` (the audits' `delta V`) exists in the graph, the chain rule
connects it to the SDC observable whether or not C23-SDC's own original argument needed to invoke
it, and the connection is informative (it gives C23-SDC a trigger law it did not have before).

**Candidate 3: two structurally distinct sources of `u_b` motion, requiring explicit coupling.**
C23-SDC's own "gamma-activation drives the brane through moduli space" language may name a
mechanism structurally different from the Goldberger-Wise brane-localized-scalar-potential
mechanism the audits use (e.g. a direct potential term for `z_0` itself, rather than a vacuum-
energy term that enters the junction condition as an effective tension shift). If so, both
mechanisms move `u_b`, but through different terms in the action, and the correct composition would
add their contributions rather than identify them. Estimated probability: **0.25**. This is the
candidate most limited by the pack-versus-graph gap noted in section 0: Paper -1's own text, not
accessible to this session beyond a truncated source-row description, is exactly the evidence that
would confirm or rule this out. Absent that text, this session cannot distinguish "gamma-activation"
naming the same Goldberger-Wise mechanism from naming something structurally separate.

## 5. Consequences for the SDC trigger, the static commitment, and S-CC-002

the enhancement factor is enormous (`~ 5 x 10^63`). The SDC trigger does not acquire a new
condition; it acquires an extreme sensitivity once the framework's own existing activation
mechanism (operator (iv), the variance coupling, per K7) is engaged.
