# physics-kernel

A typed claim graph for an active physics research program: the Displacement Geometry framework and the broader physics corpus, carried until now as a trellis with a sorry ledger and an exclusion reservoir, now seated on the [EpiStack protocol](https://github.com/A-Viable-Fork/epistack) so that its structure is mechanical rather than cultural. Every derivation, empirical constraint, killed channel, and open question is a first-class node that grounds or does not. Standing is recomputable by anyone from the public graph. A kill blocks re-entry through the gate, not through a warning an instance may skim. A conjecture cannot present as a result, because a declared grade above the earned grade is a decline, not a style violation.

This is a domain-specific kernel in the sense of the EpiStack kernel taxonomy: its own schema and floors, standing alone, built on the forked substrate. The substrate is vendored and pinned by commit and content hash (`upstream/lock.json`, verified on every build), never edited in place; deliberate divergences from upstream are recorded in the lock with rationale. The architecture rule is inherited: one trust boundary, periphery reaches the kernel only through the api, corpora is pure data, and a check derives the import graph and enforces it.

## What lives here

- `corpora/dg/` is the Displacement Geometry region: tiers, kernel constraints, functions, contracts, predictions, both cosmology branch commitments held as a crux rather than a verdict, the sorry ledger as characterized gaps with closing conditions, and the exclusion reservoir as withdrawal records with typed reinstatement conditions.
- The transformation store carries the framework's load-bearing computations, each with a mandatory assumptions manifest. A computation produces a value, never standing; landing a result as a claim is a separate typing act through the gate.
- The physics checks extend the vendored suite: the reservoir check (class-block advisory), the convexity guard (matter softens, only the vacuum stiffens), and the substrate pin.

## Status

Pre-population. The seat, the kind table, and the governing conventions land first; the DG corpus grounds in stages, each stage a committed state. The single source of build truth is `docs/status-ledger.md` once it exists; until then, the commit history is the record. The architectural reconnaissance that decided this design is `docs/physics-kernel-recon-report.md`.

## Relationship to the trellis

The authored trellis (v4.16 at seating) remains the authoring source through the grounding stages; this graph is generated from it and any divergence between the two is reported, never papered over. Whether canonicity flips to the graph is decided by running a real research shore graph-native, not by this README.

## Discipline

Gaps are first-class: every unverified node carries its closing condition, and the characterized-gaps read is the honest backlog. Generation is not verification, and every claim states which it received and from whom. No grade is raised by hand; the gate computes every standing shown. No em dashes.

## Verify

Clone, `node build/check-substrate.mjs` to confirm the vendored kernel matches the lock, then the full `build/check-*.mjs` suite. Anything a synthesis asserts about this repository is recomputable here.
