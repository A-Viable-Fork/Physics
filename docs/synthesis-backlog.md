## 1. The meta-finding

The kernel is a logic machine, not a statistics machine. Every affordance that survived the six reports is exact, ordinal, logical, and valid at small n. Every method that died imported an assumption the setting lacks: independence of errors, cardinal-valued standing, asymptotic scale, or fast unambiguous settlement. This is the sorting rule for all future tooling proposals: if a candidate method assumes independence, cardinality, or a thermodynamic limit, it is presumptively dead here until shown otherwise.

## 2. Policy amendments (land in Stage 3b, amending governing conventions)

**2.1 Independence is computed, not declared.** Two audits or evidence chains are independent iff the intersection of their minimal environments (minimal sufficient support sets over the provenance graph) is empty. The ATMS label-propagation construction (de Kleer 1986) computes these exactly at this scale; absorptive provenance polynomials (Sorp) are the equivalent algebra. This supersedes both vendor-disjointness (killed by the correlated-errors finding, PK-DR-002) and the interim method-disjointness amendment. Declared distinct-party status remains as a semantic supplement for checker identity; it no longer carries independence on its own. Where lineage is unresolved, unknown still reads as not-independent, capping lifts at checked (the Stage 2.5 rule stands).

**2.2 Ordinal discipline on the grade lattice.** Valid operations on grades are meet, join, and monotone step functions only. No averaging, no weighted sums, no compensation-style aggregation, and no cross-mode aggregation: the two incomparable modes are maintained as a product structure, processed component-wise (PK-DR-006, measurement-theory finding). Any future statistics pack proposing cardinal arithmetic over grades is refused at design time.

**2.3 Contradictions resolve by crux, never by smoothing.** The kernel-native synthesis over a held contradiction is the computed crux: the symmetric difference of the minimal environments of the two branches, which is the exact set of differing foundational commitments (converged on independently by PK-DR-004 conjunction analysis, PK-DR-005 muon g-2 intermediate-window practice, PK-DR-006 conflict cores). Statistical reconciliation of contradictory claims is dead (2.4, kill K1).

## 3. Methods kills (enter the reservoir as the kernel's first self-referential entries)

Each enters as a kill with its violated precondition as the typed reinstatement condition.

- K1: PDG-style scale-factor / statistical averaging over contradictory claims. Killed by: error correlation across LLM-generated content; the Birge-ratio machinery assumes independent inter-laboratory errors. Reinstatement: demonstration that the relevant error sources are independent (e.g. genuinely independent human-community inputs). Source: PK-DR-005.
- K2: Percolation / asymptotic robustness analysis of withdrawal cascades. Killed by: thermodynamic-limit preconditions vacuous at n in the hundreds; exact fixpoint recomputation supersedes. Reinstatement: corpus scale where exact computation is infeasible. Source: PK-DR-006.
- K3: Cardinal gradual-argumentation semantics (compensation, [0,1] weight propagation) over the grade lattice. Killed by: ordinal lattice with incomparable modes; continuous-limit convergence proofs invalid. Reinstatement: a principled cardinal refinement of standing (e.g. a future probabilistic extension), adopted deliberately at substrate level. Source: PK-DR-006.
- K4: Market-style staked verification with calibration track records. Killed by: settlement latency; staking mechanisms degenerate under slow or ambiguous settlement (replication markets required funded guaranteed settlement; bug bounties require 24-48h resolution). Reinstatement: a claim class with fast unambiguous settlement carrying enough volume to matter (recomputations and formalizations qualify individually but not at volume). Source: PK-DR-004.
- K5 (design-level, already applied): head-placed prohibition lists in generation context; schema-native generation without a scratchpad phase. Killed by: negative-constraint activation and the structured-format capacity tax. Reinstatement: model-generation evidence that these effects no longer hold. Sources: PK-DR-001, 003. Emitter spec already amended (positive constraint geometry, kills as mid-context historical data, think-first-format-later, verbalized sampling).
